About Me
More than Just a Lens

I'm a passionate visual storyteller who believes that every moment holds a unique emotion waiting to be captured. Through my lens, I transform fleeting instances into timeless memories that speak to the heart.

My approach is rooted in candid, documentary-style photography with a focus on authenticity. I don't just take pictures – I craft cinematic narratives that connect with viewers on an emotional level, whether it's the tender glance between newlyweds or the pure joy of a child's birthday celebration.

With over 5+ years of telling visual stories that connect, I've had the privilege of documenting hundreds of precious moments across India, always with the same commitment: to capture the real, unguarded emotions that make each story extraordinary.
